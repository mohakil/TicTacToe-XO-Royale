# Product Mission (Lite)

TicTacToe XO Royale is a premium mobile game that helps casual and competitive gamers enjoy a modern, high-performance Tic Tac Toe experience by providing Material 3 design, 120fps gameplay, and intelligent AI opponents.

TicTacToe XO Royale serves mobile gamers who demand smooth performance and modern design in their gaming experience. Unlike traditional Tic Tac Toe games with poor performance and outdated interfaces, TicTacToe XO Royale delivers 60-144 FPS gameplay with Material 3 design, multiple board sizes, and intelligent AI opponents across three difficulty levels.
